About

Hey, I’m Abhishek — a Staff Software Engineer focused on infrastructure, distributed systems, and the engineering craft that keeps large-scale systems running reliably.

I started this blog to write about things I find genuinely interesting.

What you’ll find here

  • Deep dives into distributed systems concepts.
  • Infrastructure engineering — Kubernetes, service meshes, platform tooling.
  • Engineering practices around reliability and scalability.
  • Occasionally, opinions on how software teams work best.

Background

I’ve spent my career building and operating systems at scale — working across compute infrastructure, data platforms, and developer tooling. I’m particularly interested in the gap between how distributed systems are described in papers and how they actually behave in production.
